We offer different reef sites across Hurghada — from mellow coral gardens to fish-packed pinnacles. Dolphins often visit these reefs.

Plateau & Drop-off

Wide ledges that lead to the blue edge — expect turtles, schooling fish, and sometimes dolphins & pelagics.

  • Depth: 10–50 m • AOW+
  • Great viz & scenery

Safari Trips & Special Dives

We offer various safari trips and special dives in Safaga and Marsa Alam — serene sea cow (dugong) meadows, thrilling shark sites, and frequent dolphin encounters.

  • Depth: 10–60 m • AOW+ (route dependent)
  • Small groups • safety-first briefings

Private Boats & Islands

We provide normal and private boat trips including islands, relaxed snorkeling, and guided diving for all levels around Hurghada.

  • Depth: 0–12 m • Snorkel & DSD friendly
